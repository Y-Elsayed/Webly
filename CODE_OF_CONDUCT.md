# Code of Conduct

We want a welcoming, inclusive community. Please be respectful and constructive.

## Our Standards
- Be kind and professional.
- Assume good intent.
- Give and receive feedback gracefully.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment or discrimination.
- Personal attacks or sustained disruption.
- Sharing private information without consent.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when needed to keep the community safe.

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the maintainer listed in `README.md`.
